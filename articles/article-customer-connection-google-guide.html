Connect with Customers to Drive Profits

In an era where digital decisions drive retail outcomes, understanding your customer’s journey is not just strategic — it’s essential. Google’s March 2024 Retail Marketing Guide, featured in Think with Google, sheds light on how businesses can connect meaningfully with customers to influence purchase behavior and increase profitability.

At the heart of this guide is a powerful truth: today’s shoppers are more mindful and more informed than ever. A full 60% of shoppers perform six or more actions before making a purchase — including comparing prices, reading reviews, and switching across channels. For marketers and retailers, this means the path to conversion is no longer linear; it’s multi-touch, multi-channel, and intent-driven.

Understanding the Modern Buyer’s Journey

  • 1 in 3 shoppers conduct additional research after discovering a product.
  • 60% of consumers take six or more steps — including reviewing brands, reading ratings, visiting physical stores, and browsing multiple websites.
  • 40% of shoppers trust Google Search most for making smart shopping decisions, compared to 34% who rely on marketplaces and just 28% on social media.

Insight: Being present at every key moment in the buyer journey is no longer optional — it’s a competitive necessity.

Be Discoverable on Google Search — The Foundation of SEO + GSO

As Google’s research emphasizes, Search is still king when it comes to influencing retail decisions. That makes a robust search strategy — both organic and paid — a core business imperative.

SEO: Search Engine Optimization Best Practices
  • Optimize for Intent: Use keyword research to align your content with what users are actually searching for.
  • Product Pages: Optimize titles, meta descriptions, schema markup, and internal linking.
  • Mobile UX: Your website must be mobile-friendly, fast, and easy to navigate.
GSO: Generative Search Optimization Essentials
  • Conversational Content: Use FAQ-style and human-like answers in product and landing pages.
  • Entity & Brand Optimization: Keep your business recognized and trusted via Google Business Profile and structured data.
  • Schema Markup: Use rich snippets for reviews, prices, and availability to boost visibility in AI-generated summaries.

Key Tip: GSO helps your brand show up in AI-generated shopping results — where modern users are beginning their research.

️Build a Unified Omnichannel Experience

Modern consumers expect consistent, personalized interactions — whether they’re engaging on mobile, desktop, in-store, or across social platforms.

Use Google’s Toolset:
  • Performance Max Campaigns: Reach users across Search, Display, YouTube, Discover, and Gmail.
  • YouTube Ads: Share product videos, reviews, and demos to build trust and engagement.
  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): Track cross-channel behavior and optimize accordingly.

Case Example: A fashion retailer using Performance Max and GSO increased ROAS by 27% in Q1 2024 by optimizing landing pages for conversational queries and leveraging YouTube Shorts for engagement.

Strategic Recommendations for Retailers

  • Invest in Visibility: Prioritize SEO and GSO to dominate organic and generative search.
  • Advertise Smartly: Use data-driven Google Ads campaigns that target users across devices and platforms.
  • Simplify the Journey: Ensure product discovery, comparison, and purchase are seamless across touchpoints.
  • Be Present Where It Matters: Focus on Google Search — not just social media.

Conclusion

The 2024 Think with Google report confirms a reality retailers can no longer ignore: buying decisions are complex, deliberate, and digitally guided. Your brand needs to show up — consistently and meaningfully — at every step.

That means building smarter SEO foundations, embracing the next generation of Generative Search Optimization, and creating seamless omnichannel journeys that meet users where they are.
